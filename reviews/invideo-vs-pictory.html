InVideo AI vs Pictory 2026: Cheaper and Faster — But Pictory Still Wins One Use Case

Updated 2026-07-13 · Every claim source-linked below · Evidence: medium

InVideo AI and Pictory both turn a script or prompt into a finished, captioned video — but they're built for different buyers. We compared price, templates, voiceovers, and plan limits to sort out which one actually fits your use case.

Verdict
Recommended — with caveats — InVideo AI · 4/5
Cheaper, more videos, stronger short-form templates
Best for
Creators spinning up frequent faceless short-form videos from prompts on a budget
Biggest strength
Cheaper per video with templates built for short-form output
Biggest weakness
Stock-footage matching can be less accurate than Pictory's
Pricing catch
Pictory costs more yet gives half the monthly videos (30 vs 60), capped at 10 minutes.
Avoid if
You mainly repurpose blogs and long videos into shorts wanting footage-matching
Better alternative
Pictory — Repurposing existing blogs and long videos into captioned shorts with accurate footage
Verdict
Budget short-form creators pick InVideo AI; repurposers pick Pictory
