Fair questions

How does The AI Shortlist make money?

Through affiliate partnerships with some of the tools we review, and through clearly-labeled sponsorships. Affiliate links never decide a verdict — our picks are made on the evidence first, and we routinely name free alternatives that pay us nothing.

Do vendors pay to be ranked?

No. Rankings and verdicts cannot be bought. Sponsored placements, when they exist, are labeled and kept separate from ranked verdicts — see our editorial standards.

How do you test tools?

Each review starts from one buyer question, synthesizes the strongest expert evidence and hands-on tests for that category, verifies claims against their sources, and ends with one clear pick plus the honest alternative where one exists.

Why should I trust a site with affiliate links?

Judge us on the calls that cost us money: we name free alternatives to paid picks in the verdict itself. A recommendation you can't trust is worth nothing — trust is the entire business model.
